About Paycheck to Paycheck

What is Paycheck to Paycheck?

Paycheck to Paycheck is an open-source bill management system created to help individuals track and manage their bills with ease. The project is designed for anyone who lives paycheck to paycheck and needs a simple, effective way to stay on top of due dates and balances.

This service is completely free and open-source, available for anyone to use. The goal is to help users manage their financial obligations and avoid the stress of missing payment deadlines.

You can explore the project’s source code on GitHub: GitHub Repository

About the Creator

Hello! My name is Anthony House, and I’m the creator of Paycheck to Paycheck. I graduated from Iowa State University in 2019 with a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, along with minors in Entrepreneurial Studies and General Business.

I’m passionate about software development and have honed my skills in various areas, including:

  • Docker
  • Serverless Architecture
  • Frontend & Backend Development
  • AWS Infrastructure

I created Paycheck to Paycheck because, like many Americans, I understand the challenges of living paycheck to paycheck. My aim is to provide a simple, effective tool that helps people keep track of their bills and avoid the stress of missed payments.

While there are no immediate plans for the future development of this project, I am proud to offer it as a free service for anyone in need.
